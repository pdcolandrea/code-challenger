About

Description

Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn, usually simply known as Rembrandt, was a Dutch Golden Age painter, printmaker, and draughtsman. He is generally considered one of the greatest visual artists in the history of Western art. Wikipedia
Born: July 15, 1606, Leiden, Netherlands
Died: October 4, 1669 (age 63 years), Amsterdam, Netherlands
Periods: Baroque, Baroque painting, Dutch Golden Age
Spouse: Saskia van Uylenburgh (m. 1634–1642)
Children: Titus van Rijn, Cornelia van Rijn, Rumbartus van Rijn
Full name: Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn
